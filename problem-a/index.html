Isaac Moriah

Isaac Moriah, mainly refered to as "Isaac", is the protagionist in the popular roguelike game The Binding of Isaac and is one of the playable characters in the series. He is illustrated as a young, crying child who is bald and naked, whose story involves his escape from his mother's murderous intents.

Reasons that this character is admirable

  • Very memorable character in the gaming community; has many memes and emotes made in reference to the character and the series
  • Representative of the impacts that parenting has on the development of children; resembles the dark thoughts that children face when under abusive/undervalued circumstances
  • Ingame starts with the "D6" when unlocked, which gives the player the ability to reroll underwelming items into possibly game-winning items
